FORCAM GmbH
Since October 2020, I work for FORCAM GmbH in Ravensburg. Started as a Software Developer, I have been able to introduce and build up the area of user experience research here since May 2022. This includes the definition and implementation of a UX process in line with existing agile software development practices as well as the selection of appropriate research methods for different stages of product development. The design, execution, analysis and interpretation of the resulting quantitative, qualitative and mixed-methods studies are among my tasks. The results of my work are directly incorporated into further product development. In addition, I design and conduct training courses in the area of user experience especially for for software developers and act as a mentor for UI and UX designers and product and product managers in the area of UX research.
